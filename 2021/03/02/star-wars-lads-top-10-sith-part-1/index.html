Star Wars Lads: Top 10 Sith Part 1!

Posted on March 2, 2021March 4, 2021

To counter last week’s two-parter episode, this week’s two-parter from the Star Wars Lads is all about the Top 10 Sith! It’s a bit harder to do a full canon Top 10 of Sith, so we expanded it to more of a “Dark Side Users” list, so we were able to fit in some great characters from both Canon and Legends that aren’t strictly “Sith.” We give our 10-6 rankings in this first part! Hope y’all enjoy, and tune in for the second part coming out on Friday! Through victory, our chains are broken!
